Biography

Bogdan Stevanovic is a young and ambitious youngster who has been fascinated by computers since the age of three, when he began playing games on them. Born in Bujanovac, where there was no place for him to learn about subjects that piqued his interest, he was obliged to study from the best source on the internet, Google. When he relocated to Nis, he gained access to both education and social life. He participated in computer science in elementary school, which influenced his decision to enroll in the computer department at Bora Stankovic High School. He decided to attend the Faculty of Electronics after graduating from high school's computer department.
